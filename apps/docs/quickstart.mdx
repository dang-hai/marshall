# Quickstart

Get Marshall running on your machine.

## Prerequisites

- macOS (Apple Silicon or Intel)
- Microphone access for call transcription

## Installation

Download the latest release from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/dang-hai/marshall/releases).

1. Download the `.dmg` file for your architecture
2. Open the DMG and drag Marshall to Applications
3. Launch Marshall and grant microphone permissions

## First Call

1. Open Marshall before joining your call
2. Start your meeting in your preferred app (Zoom, Meet, etc.)
3. Marshall will automatically detect audio and begin monitoring
4. View real-time insights in the Marshall window
