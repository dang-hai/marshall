# Welcome to Marshall

Marshall helps lead focused calls. It acts like a moderator that makes sure your calls do not derail and that you capture the information you wanted to catch in the first place.

## Features

- **Active Monitoring** — Marshall actively monitors your calls in real-time
- **Proactive Support** — Get nudges and suggestions to keep discussions on track
- **Information Capture** — Never miss important points from your meetings
